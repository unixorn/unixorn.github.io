Back up your Talos etcd cluster to a smb share

In this post, I will show how to access smb shares outside the cluster from a Kubernetes Pod. I will be backing up the etcd cluster in my Talos k8s cluster to a share, but you can use this for any service (like Plex or Jellyfin) that need access to files on a NAS.
